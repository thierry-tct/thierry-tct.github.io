Biography

Thierry Titcheu Chekam is a Software engineer at SES. Prior to that, he was a postdoctoral Researcher/Research Associate at the Interdisciplinary Center for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT) of the University of Luxembourg, where he also completed his PhD. His PhD thesis received the FNR outstanding PhD thesis award for the year 2020. His research interests include software testing and analysis, Cloud storage. He was recipient of the AFR-PhD grand from Luxembourg’s national research fund (FNR) which graciously sponsored his Doctoral research. He was also recipient of the Chinese Scholarship Council (CSC) cooperation scholarship between Cameroon and China during his Masters and Bachelors degree studies.
